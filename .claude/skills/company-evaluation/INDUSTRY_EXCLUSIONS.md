# Industry Exclusion List

Companies in these industries are automatically excluded (recommend PASS) regardless of other factors.

---

## Hard Exclusions (Immediate PASS)

### Major Banks & Traditional Banking
- Chase, Bank of America, Wells Fargo, Citi
- Regional banks (PNC, US Bank, TD Bank, etc.)
- Investment banks (Goldman Sachs, Morgan Stanley, JP Morgan)
- Credit unions over $10B assets

**Why Excluded**: Bureaucratic culture, slow pace, regulatory burden, outdated technology

### Government & Federal Contractors
- Any federal, state, or local government agency
- Federal contractors (Lockheed Martin, Raytheon, Booz Allen, etc.)
- Companies requiring security clearance
- Contractors for DoD, DHS, FBI, CIA, NSA

**Why Excluded**: Bureaucracy, red tape, limited technology choices, ethical concerns

### Defense & Military Contractors
- Weapons manufacturers
- Defense systems companies
- Military technology providers
- Companies with >50% defense revenue

**Why Excluded**: Ethical concerns, slow pace, security constraints

### Traditional Insurance
- Property & casualty insurance
- Life insurance companies
- Legacy insurance administration
- Exception: Insurtech startups with modern stacks

**Why Excluded**: Legacy systems, regulatory burden, slow innovation

### Gambling & Casinos
- Casino operators
- Sports betting platforms
- Online gambling companies
- Lottery systems

**Why Excluded**: Ethical concerns, regulatory complexity

### Tobacco & Vaping
- Cigarette manufacturers
- Vape/e-cigarette companies
- Tobacco distribution

**Why Excluded**: Ethical concerns

### Speculative Crypto/Web3
- Pure crypto trading platforms
- NFT marketplaces
- Web3-only companies with no real utility
- Exception: Blockchain infrastructure with real use cases

**Why Excluded**: Instability, regulatory uncertainty, often vaporware

---

## Proceed with Caution

These industries are not excluded but require careful evaluation:

### Healthcare (Traditional)
- Large hospital systems
- Legacy healthcare IT
- Heavy HIPAA burden environments

**Concerns**: Regulatory complexity, legacy systems
**Exceptions**: Innovative healthtech, modern EHR startups

### Big Tech / FAANG
- Google, Meta, Amazon, Apple, Microsoft
- Other large tech companies (>10,000 employees)

**Concerns**: Bureaucracy at scale, limited impact
**Exceptions**: Specific teams with exceptional culture/mission

### Fintech (Traditional)
- Payment processors
- Legacy financial services

**Concerns**: Regulatory burden, compliance overhead
**Exceptions**: Modern fintech with good culture (Stripe, Plaid, etc.)

---

## Tier 1 Industries (Preferred)

These industries are most desirable:

- **AI/ML Platforms**: Building AI infrastructure and tools
- **Developer Tools**: IDEs, DevOps, infrastructure
- **B2B SaaS**: Enterprise software
- **E-commerce/Marketplace**: Platforms and tools
- **Climate Tech**: Sustainability and clean energy

---

## Evaluation Override

Even in preferred industries, still evaluate:
- Company-specific culture
- Leadership quality
- Remote/flexibility policies
- Compensation alignment

A Tier 1 industry company with poor culture still gets a low score.
