# Role-Specific Resume Adaptations

## Quick Reference: Which Adaptation to Use

| Target Role | Primary Focus | Emphasize | De-emphasize |
|-------------|---------------|-----------|--------------|
| Full-Stack Developer | Rapid delivery, production apps | Shipping speed, end-to-end | Pure management |
| AI Engineer | Production AI, LLMs, RAG | LLM experience, AI products | Frontend-only work |
| Frontend Engineer | React/Next.js, UX, performance | UI/UX, performance metrics | Backend, infrastructure |
| Technical Lead | Team leadership, mentoring | Team management, delivery | Solo projects |
| Senior Software Engineer | Breadth of experience | Range and depth | Nothing - show breadth |
| Founding Engineer | 0-to-1 building, autonomy | Startup experience | Enterprise process |
